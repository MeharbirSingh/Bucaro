Easy Ordering

How it works

Ordering at BÚCARO is quick and easy. Follow these three simple steps and we'll take care of the rest.

1

Choose your food & drinks

Browse our menu or look at the display case and choose everything you'd like to enjoy.

2

Tell us your table number

Go to the cashier and let us know your table number while placing your order.

3

We serve your table

We prepare everything fresh and quickly bring the hot dishes directly to your table.
